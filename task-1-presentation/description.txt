Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness
Created a visual guide using Canva to help batchmates understand the four pillars of digital literacy: Awareness, Security, Professional Presence, and Critical Thinking.
  Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Tool Used: Canva
Design Overview: For the first task of my Student Digital Ambassador assignment, I designed a one-page visual resource titled "Digital Literacy Guide". The infographic is structured into four distinct phases to help my peers navigate the digital landscape effectively.
•
Phase 1: Awareness focuses on defining digital literacy as the ability to find and evaluate information beyond just using a smartphone.
•
Phase 2: Security highlights essential safety practices, such as using strong passwords and enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
•
Phase 3: Presence emphasizes the importance of a professional digital footprint on platforms like GitHub or LinkedIn.
•
Phase 4: Critical Thinking addresses the responsibility of fact-checking to stop the spread of misinformation.
Reflections: I found the process of condensing complex topics into short, actionable bullet points quite interesting, as it forced me to think about what information is most vital for a student's safety. One thing I found difficult was selecting a layout that looked professional yet engaging for my batchmates. Ultimately, using Canva allowed me to create a high-quality resource that serves as a foundational guide for anyone looking to improve their digital literacy.
